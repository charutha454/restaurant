ABOUT US


Wendy's is an American international fast-food restaurant chain founded by Dave Thomas on November 15, 1969,
in Columbus, Ohio. Known for its square-shaped hamburgers, sea salt fries, and Frosty desserts, Wendy's sets itself apart with a commitment to quality ingredients and fresh preparation.
The brand emphasizes its "Quality is our Recipe" motto, offering a menu that includes chicken sandwiches, salads,
and other sides. Wendy's has grown to become one of the largest fast-food chains globally, with thousands of locations across the world, maintaining a strong presence in the fast-food industry through innovation and customer satisfaction.
